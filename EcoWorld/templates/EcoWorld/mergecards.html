What is merging cards?

This page is used to upgrade your cards.

It works by adding 5 cards of the same rarity to upgrade into 1 card of the superior rarity.

For example, doing 5 common bushes will gift you 1 random rare card.

Merging 5 legendary cards will gift you a mythical card not available in the store!
